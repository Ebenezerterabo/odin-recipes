Indomie Noodles

Indomie is an instant noodle brand produced by the indonesian company indofood. Indomie is among the leading instant noodle manufacturers in the world, with 17 factories.

Over 19 billion packs of Indomie are produced annually, and exported to more than 80 countries.Indomie has been produced primarily in Indonesia since its launch in 1972. It has also been produced in Nigeria since 1995, Turkey since 2010, and Serbia since 2016

Steps

  1. Cut the indomie indomie into pieces and place it in a bowl with boiling water. Allow the noodles to soften for about five minutes
  2. Preheat your frying pan, then add the sliced tomatoes, onions, and fresh pepper and cook for another five minutes. After 4 minutes, drain the water from the water from the noodles and add the remaining spices while still in the dish; the noodles will be soft and hot at this stage.
  3. Stir the two eggs together in the same bowl with the noodles before transferring to the pan. The fried tomatoes, onions, and pepper are in the frying pan; heat and stir for about five minutes, and you're done.
