Fried Rice

Fried rice is a tasty rice dish made by cooking rice and then frying it with vegetable and seasonings. Common ingredients include carrots, green peas, sweet corn, onions, and sometimes eggs or meat. It has a light color and a pleasant smell that makes it appealing.

Fried rice is often served at homes and special events. It is usually eaten with chicken, fish, or salad. Many people enjoy fried rice because it is colorful, easy to eat, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Onions
  • Cooking oil
