Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice cooked in a rich tomato sauce. The sauce is prepared using tomatoes, onions, pepper, and simple spices, which give the dish its bright color and tasty flavor. it is often cooked in one pot, allowing the rice to absorb all the flavors.

Jollof rice is commonly served at homes, parties, and special occasions. It can be eaten on its own or with chicken, fish, beef, or salad. Many people enjoy jollof because it is filling, colorful, and enjoyable to eat.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, onions, and pepper until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the blended tomatoes, onions, and pepper, and cook for about five minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the rice and stir to combine. Cook for another five minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is cooked through and the sauce is thickened.
  4. Cover and cook until the rice is soft and well flavored.
